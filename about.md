Hi! I’m Berat Onur Ersen.

I’m using this site to log my activities, thoughts, things those matter to me.

If we were living in the same place, you would probably see me playing guitar, sometimes searching for something 
interesting on the internet, often learning&coding something, then if you’re lucky I would be making a pizza for you :)

In the past, I was very active on social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram etc. but now I prefer to 
concentrate on living life real rather living a digital form.

I admit social networks are very effective in people’s lives, choices… but if you want to be unique, if you plan to 
create memories for yourselves, with your loved ones those matter to you but no one then it’s better to put down that phone, tablet what-so-ever.

I set this blog’s name as “Boogie Woogie by Onur” because I like playing guitar in the blues style. 
If I was not an engineer, I would like to make music for a living but now we are here, who knows maybe my next take on life will be that :)
